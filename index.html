Famous Jigarthanda

Must try

Jigarthanda is a famous drink unique to Madurai. It is prepared using Milk, China grass, Sarsaparilla syrup and Ice-cream.

When to visit?

Whenever you feel like indulging in a creamy cold dessert!

Remarks

We would advise you to get it in a disposable cup to avoid the crowd and for better hygiene.

Chandran mess

Non.Veg

There are no vegetarian food options available here. However, you get a wide variety of chicken, mutton and seafood options. They offer almost 6-8 varieties of curry to go with the rice.

What to buy?

Nenju kari roast(a tender and fatty mutton dish), raatu(prawns), crab omelette, kola urundai(meat balls).

Remarks

Your waiter may serve meat from the curry, without your acknowledgement and will charge you for it, so order meat from the “what to buy” section instead.

Konar kadai

Non.Veg

There are two konar kadais: one in Simmakkal and the other in Anna nagar. If you are closer to the wedding venue, you can visit the Anna nagar shop and if you are closer to Meenakshi Amman temple, you can visit the Simmakkal shop.

What to buy?

Kari dosai(signature meat dosai) which we recommend you share between 2-3 people, yennai dosai + egg poriyal.

Remarks

Please note that the Simmakkal branch may be a bit noisy and crowded.
